Create Beautiful Timelapses!

Tutorial

Step 1: Choose a scenic location or subject that keeps changing through time.

Step 2: Set up your camera or phone on a tripod, select the best angle to capture the time-lapse, and configure camera settings like shutter speed, interval and focus. Then start recording and let your camera take multiple images at regular intervals!

Step 3: Use a video editing software to compile your time-lapse video(s) into a cohesive and visually appealing video. Add music, transitions, color correction, and other effects to enhance the final product.
